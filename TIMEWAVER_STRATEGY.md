# 🎵 Strategic Report: TimeWaver Audio Frequency Online Business

## 1. TimeWaver & Healy Technical Feasibility

**Core Question:** Can the hardware generate sellable digital assets?

### A. Digital Audio Generation (The "Product")

- **Native Capability:** TimeWaver and Healy devices primarily output frequencies via **microcurrent** (electrodes) or **magnetic field** (coil). They are _not_ synthesizers designed to export high-fidelity `.wav` or `.mp3` music files directly.
- **The Workaround (Digital Product Strategy):**
  - **Option 1: The "Infused" Audio:** You cannot "record" the microcurrent directly into a song. Instead, practitioners often use a **Quantum Potential Frequencies (QPF)** approach. You create a high-quality ambient track (using standard DAWs like Logic/Ableton) and run the TimeWaver "on top" of the master file during export, or market the audio as "frequency-embedded" via intention (radionics principle).
  - **Option 2: Binaural/Isochronic Tones:** Use standard audio software to generate the _audible_ equivalent of the TimeWaver frequencies detected (e.g., if TimeWaver detects a need for 432Hz, you generate a 432Hz tone in software).
  - **Verdict:** You cannot simply hit "Export MP3" on a TimeWaver. The product must be a hybrid of **standard audio production + TimeWaver analysis data**.

### B. Automated PDF Reports

- **Native Capability:** TimeWaver Pro/Frequency allows for PDF export of analysis results.
- **Automation:** It is **not** API-friendly. It is legacy Windows software.
- **The Workflow:** You cannot fully automate this (e.g., User pays -> API triggers TimeWaver -> Email sends PDF).
- **Solution:** **"Semi-Automated Service Bureau"** model.
  1.  Orders collect in a dashboard (Notion/Airtable).
  2.  A human operator runs the batch scans once per day.
  3.  Operator uploads PDFs to the dashboard, which triggers the email dispatch.

## 5. Automation & Tech Stack

Since TimeWaver has no API, we build a **"Human-in-the-Loop"** automation.

### Delivery Stack

1.  **Storefront:** **Shopify** or **Teachable** (for digital downloads).
    - _Why:_ Handles VAT, digital delivery, and secure payments easily.
2.  **Order Management:** **Airtable** or **Notion**.
    - _Workflow:_ Zapier captures new order -> Adds row to Airtable "To Scan" view -> Operator runs scan -> Uploads PDF to Airtable field -> Zapier detects change -> Emails file to customer.
3.  **Audio Hosting:** **SoundCloud (Private)** or **Vimeo (Audio only)**.
    - _Security:_ Don't just send MP3s they can share. Send a link to a private player.

### Marketing Stack

1.  **Email/Line:** **ActiveCampaign** or **ConvertKit**.
    - _Line OA (Taiwan):_ Essential for customer support and "Daily Frequency" broadcast messages.
2.  **Social:** Instagram/TikTok (Visuals of the TimeWaver interface "scanning" are very viral).
